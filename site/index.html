One click to save every tab.

SonTab is a lightweight, OneTab-style tab manager for Chrome. Collect the whole window into a group, free your memory, and find everything again — by time or by site.

Everything, one click away

Tabs are closed only after they are safely stored — nothing is ever lost.

🗂

Catalog view

Groups sorted by creation time, with inline renaming, locking, and per-tab or per-group restore.

🔎

Instant search

Live search across titles and URLs, plus Starred and Locked-groups filters.

⭐

Starred tabs

Pages you keep coming back to stay in the list even after being restored.

🧹

Less clutter

Home pages like google.com are hidden automatically — starred tabs excepted, and you can turn it off.

🔄

OneTab import

Bring your OneTab data — text export or a saved page — with group names and original dates preserved.

🎨

Easy on the eyes

A warm paper-archive design with auto, light, and dark themes in five languages.

See your tabs by site

The by-domain view gathers every saved tab per site, shows which group each one came from, and keeps long lists tidy with a configurable newest-first limit.

Private by design

No account. No server. No analytics. Everything is stored locally on your device and never leaves it. Read the privacy policy.
