# SonTab Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 16, 2026_

SonTab is a browser extension that saves and restores your tabs. It is designed to be private by default.

## What data SonTab handles

When you click the SonTab toolbar icon, the extension reads the **URL, title, and favicon** of the tabs in your current window in order to save them as a group. If you use the import feature, the URLs and titles contained in the imported file or text are also stored. Your settings (theme, language, display options) are stored as well.

## Where the data is stored

**All data stays on your device.** Everything is stored locally in your browser's extension storage (`chrome.storage.local`).

- SonTab has **no backend server**.
- SonTab makes **no network requests**.
- SonTab includes **no analytics, telemetry, or tracking** of any kind.
- Your data is **never transmitted, sold, or shared** with anyone.

## Permissions

- **tabs** — required to read the URL/title of open tabs when you save them and to reopen tabs when you restore them.
- **storage / unlimitedStorage** — required to keep your saved groups and settings on your device without size limits.

## Data deletion

Deleting a tab or group inside SonTab removes it from local storage immediately. Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please open an issue at
https://github.com/MartianLee/sontab/issues.

# SonTab 개인정보처리방침 (Korean)

SonTab은 탭을 저장·복원하는 브라우저 확장 프로그램으로, 기본적으로 프라이버시를 보호하도록 설계되었습니다.

- 툴바 아이콘을 누를 때 현재 창 탭의 **URL·제목·파비콘**을 읽어 그룹으로 저장하며, 설정(테마·언어·표시 옵션)도 함께 저장됩니다.
- **모든 데이터는 사용자의 기기에만** (`chrome.storage.local`) 저장됩니다.
- 백엔드 서버가 없고, 네트워크 요청을 하지 않으며, 분석·추적 코드가 전혀 없습니다. 데이터를 외부로 전송·판매·공유하지 않습니다.
- SonTab 안에서 탭/그룹을 삭제하면 즉시 로컬 저장소에서 제거되며, 확장 프로그램을 삭제하면 모든 데이터가 삭제됩니다.
